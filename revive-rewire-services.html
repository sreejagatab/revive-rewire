Electrical Installation Condition Reports (EICRs)

An EICR (Electrical Installation Condition Report) is a comprehensive inspection of your property's electrical systems and components to ensure they meet legal safety standards and are operating correctly.

Our thorough EICR service provides peace of mind for homeowners and helps landlords meet their legal obligations to tenants.

  • Comprehensive inspection of all electrical systems
  • Identification of potential electrical hazards
  • Official certification for landlords and homeowners
  • Detailed report with clear recommendations
  • Fast turnaround times

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our electrical services.

How long does a full house rewire take?

The duration of a full house rewire depends on the size and complexity of your property. For an average 3-bedroom house, a rewire typically takes 7-10 days. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily life and can discuss scheduling options to accommodate your needs.

How often should I get an EICR?

For homeowners, we recommend getting an EICR every 10 years or when you purchase a new property. For landlords, regulations require an EICR every 5 years or when there's a change of tenancy. Properties with swimming pools or high risk factors may need more frequent inspections.

What type of EV chargers do you install?

We install a range of quality EV chargers from leading manufacturers. We primarily recommend and install home charging units with outputs ranging from 3.6kW to 22kW depending on your vehicle and property's electrical capacity. During our assessment, we'll recommend the most suitable charger for your specific needs.

Do you offer any guarantees on your work?

Yes, all our electrical work comes with a comprehensive guarantee. Full rewires and major electrical installations are guaranteed for 6 years, while smaller installations and repairs carry a 12-month guarantee. Additionally, we provide all necessary certification for insurance purposes.

Are you registered with any professional bodies?

Yes, Revive Rewire is fully certified and registered with relevant professional bodies. Our electricians are qualified to current standards, and we maintain all necessary insurance and certifications to ensure our work meets industry regulations and safety standards.
