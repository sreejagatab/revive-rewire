Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our electrical services.

How quickly can you respond to my enquiry?

We aim to respond to all enquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call us directly on 07903 889841 for the fastest response.

Do you provide free quotations?

Yes, we provide free no-obligation quotes for all our electrical services. We can give ballpark figures over the phone, but for accurate pricing, we typically need to visit your property to assess the specific requirements.

What areas do you cover?

We primarily serve Greater Manchester and surrounding areas, including Stockport, Salford, Bolton, Bury, Oldham, Rochdale, Wigan, and parts of Cheshire. For locations outside these areas, please contact us to discuss your requirements.

How far in advance should I book for electrical work?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance for non-urgent electrical work, especially for larger projects like full rewires. However, we understand that emergencies happen, and we always try to accommodate urgent requests when possible.
